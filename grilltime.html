Propane Tank Remaining Usage Calculator

I realized that I was probably replacing the propane tank on our grill with a full tank before we really needed to because I was not sure how much longer it would last so I found out how to calculate the remaining time. This is a very simple way to determine how much remaining time you have in the grill propane tank. You need to know 3 numbers to do it:
  • grill BTUs (how much gas it burns per hour), 12000 is a typical value
  • empty tank weight, this should be written next to TW (tare weight) on the collar/top of the propane tank, 16.6 is typical
  • current tank weight, get a bathroom scale and put the tank on it
